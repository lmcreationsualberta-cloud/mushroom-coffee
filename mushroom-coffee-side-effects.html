Mushroom coffee is among the safest functional beverages available. For the vast majority of healthy adults there are no meaningful side effects. That said there are specific situations that warrant caution and we cover all of them here honestly.

What to Know Before You Start

Digestive Adjustment Period

The most commonly reported experience in the first week is mild digestive discomfort. Medicinal mushrooms contain high concentrations of beta-glucan fibers which act as prebiotics. Starting with a half serving and building up over a week eliminates this for most people.

Medication Interactions

Chaga may interact with blood thinners including warfarin. Reishi can amplify the effects of blood pressure and blood sugar medications. If you take any prescription medications consult your doctor before adding mushroom coffee to your routine.

Who Should Avoid It

Pregnant and breastfeeding women should avoid medicinal mushrooms due to insufficient safety research. People with known mushroom or mold allergies should also consult a physician first.
